About Ledebe

We built the tool we wished existed — a simple way to work with AI without exposing sensitive data.

Why Ledebe Exists

AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini are transforming how people work. But there is a problem nobody talks about enough — to use these tools effectively, people paste in real emails, real names, real phone numbers, real documents. Sensitive data leaves devices and enters AI systems every day, often without users realising the risk.

Ledebe was built to fix that. It lets you work with AI freely — without exposing the data that isn't yours to share.

The core idea is simple: mask the sensitive parts before they leave your device. Use AI freely with the masked version. Restore the original whenever you need it.

What We Build

Ledebe is a suite of privacy tools for individuals, teams, and organisations who work with AI daily.

  • ✓ Desktop app — available on Mac, Windows, and Linux. Works offline, no account required.
  • ✓ VS Code extension — protect code, configs, and logs directly inside your editor.
  • ✓ File protection — upload PDFs, Word documents, and images to scan and mask PII before sharing.

Our Principles

Local first

All detection and masking happens on your device. Your data never touches our servers.

Fully reversible

Masked data can always be restored. You're never locked out of your own content.

No tracking

We don't collect what you protect. No analytics on your content, ever.

Built for everyone

From solo developers to enterprise compliance teams — Ledebe works for all.
