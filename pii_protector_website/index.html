How it works

Three steps to safe AI use

No setup, no configuration. Paste, protect, and share — in seconds.

1

Paste your text

Paste any text, document, or code that contains sensitive information into Ledebe.

2

Ledebe masks it

All PII is replaced with placeholders like [LDB_EMAIL1] locally on your device — nothing leaves.

3

Share safely

Copy the protected text into any AI tool. Restore the original anytime with one click.

Features

Everything you need to use AI safely

Built for individuals, developers, and teams.

Automatic PII Detection

Instantly detects and masks emails, phone numbers, NI numbers, credit cards, API keys, and more — no manual selection needed.

Custom Terms

Add your own sensitive words — company names, project codes, client names — and they're masked automatically every time.

Fully Reversible

Placeholders map back to your original data. Restore the full text anytime — nothing is ever lost.

Document Upload

Upload PDFs, Word docs, images, and CSV files. PII is masked across the full document with a side-by-side preview.

Ask AI Mode

Send your masked text directly to ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini using your own API key — your real data never reaches the AI.

Highlight to Protect

Highlight any word in a sent message to instantly protect or unprotect it. Changes apply immediately — no re-sending needed.

Company Sync

Share a protected terms list across your whole team. One admin update protects everyone instantly.

Image OCR

Extract and protect text from images automatically. Perfect for scanned documents, screenshots, and photos.

Keyboard First

Full keyboard navigation with ⌘K search, ⌘⇧N new chat, and ⌘↵ to send. Built for speed.

Your data never leaves your device

Every piece of PII detection and masking happens locally — in your browser or desktop app. Ledebe has no server that sees your content.

  • Local processing — masking happens on your device, not our servers
  • API keys stored locally only — never uploaded to Ledebe
  • Chat history saved in your browser's local storage only
  • GDPR compliant — no personal data processed by Ledebe
  • Fully reversible — your original data is never destroyed
