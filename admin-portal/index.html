How Team Sync Works
  1. Add protected terms in the Protected Terms tab. Terms are encrypted on your device before being uploaded.
  2. Share the company join code with your team (see Team Members tab).
  3. Each team member enters the code in their Ledebe app. Their device uses the code to decrypt the team key locally.
  4. The app fetches encrypted terms from the server and decrypts them locally every 5 minutes.
  5. Ledebe's servers never see your plaintext terms — they only store encrypted data.
